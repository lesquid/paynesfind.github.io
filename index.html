About

Discover the Secrets Beneath the Surface

Step aboard Paynes Find, a 10.4m high-speed commercial charter vessel built for adventure. Purpose-designed for research, film, and diving operations, she now offers unforgettable scenic and dive charters through one of Australia's most pristine coastal frontiers—the Esperance Archipelago of the Recherche.

Owned and operated by seasoned ocean explorer Marc Payne, Paynes Find is more than just a boat—it's a gateway to hidden coves, crystal-clear waters, and marine life rarely seen by the everyday traveler. With decades of experience working above and below the waterline, Marc brings a unique depth of knowledge, storytelling, and passion to every journey.

Whether you're chasing untouched dive sites, filming remote ocean wildlife, or simply soaking in the raw beauty of the Southern Ocean, Paynes Find will take you there—fast, safe, and in style.

Dive in. Explore deeper. The treasures of the Recherche await.

The Boat – Paynes Find

Paynes Find is available for vessel hire only, offering unique access to Western Australia's stunning coastal and offshore waters.

When she’s not supporting research or filming expeditions, Paynes Find is available for private charter—perfect for small group adventures with a spirit for the sea.

  • Day trips: Up to 9 guests on coastal adventures
  • Offshore trips: Up to 6 guests
  • Overnight liveaboards: Sleeps up to 4 comfortably

She’s built for range and reliability, capable of reaching remote islands and untouched locations for those ready to take their adventure to the next level.

Film and Research

Marc’s passion for marine research was sparked in 1979, when his father dived the Great Australian Bight. Since then, Marc has forged a reputation as one of Australia’s most experienced and respected marine operators. He has played key roles in dozens of discoveries and collaborated with some of the world’s leading researchers and film crews.

With decades of hands-on experience, Marc has worked across a range of high-profile marine research and film productions, serving as a Commercial Dive Supervisor, Vessel Master, and Marine Operations Coordinator. His work is known for its precision, safety, and reliability—whether managing complex logistics at sea or diving remote, challenging locations.
